A knowledge-exchange initiative uniting vascular surgeons from Egypt with the renowned specialists in Kiel, Germany. The collaboration focuses on enhancing medical education, pioneering innovative surgical techniques, and improving patient outcomes.

About the Collaboration

University Hospital Schleswig-Holstein (UKSH) is one of Europe's largest medical centers, providing maximum and highly differentiated care. It uniquely integrates healthcare, research, and teaching to directly apply scientific findings. As the largest employer in the state, it operates over 85 clinics with 16,000 staff. Strategic personnel development and work-life balance are core to its culture.
