Message from the Board

We are delighted to inform you about a remarkable collaboration between Vascular Surgery Department-Evangelical Hospital in Bergisch Gladbach, as an academic hospital affiliated with the University of Cologne headed by Professor Payman Majd & Vascular Surgery Departments in whole Egyptian hospitals mainly Cairo university hospitals.

This cooperation is created and endorsed by ‚ÄúEgyptian Vascular Academy (EVA)‚Äù presented by Professor M. Sharkawy. This innovative program allows the vascular surgeons to benefit from the expertise and knowledge of the vascular surgeons in Bergisch Gladbach- Germany.

The aim of this collaboration is to enhance the training and education of vascular surgeons, providing them with the opportunity to learn from international best practices. Through the exchange of knowledge, experiences and surgical techniques, the vascular surgeons are empowered to deliver exceptional care to their patients.

The Evangelical Hospital in Bergisch Gladbach boasts a renowned Vascular Surgery Department, affiliated with the University of Cologne as an academic hospital. The experts in Bergisch Gladbach are highly qualified and possess vast experience in vascular surgery Headed by a great Scientist Vascular Sugeon & Pioneer interventionist Professor Payman

The exchange between the Vascular Surgery Department in whole Egypt, specially Cairo University and the Vascular Surgery Department at the Evangelical Hospital in Bergisch Gladbach is a significant step towards promoting global collaboration and enhancing medical care in the field of vascular surgery. By exchanging experiences and working together on research projects, new insights are gained, and innovative treatment methods are developed.

This collaboration serves as an outstanding example of how cross-border cooperation and the exchange of medical expertise can elevate patient care to a higher level. We take pride in the joint efforts of the Vascular Surgery Department at Cairo University and the Vascular Surgery Department at the Evangelical Hospital in Bergisch Gladbach to continuously improve medical care in the field of vascular surgery.

We are confident that this collaboration will continue to enable vascular surgeons from both institutions to expand their knowledge and enhance their skills. Ultimately, it is the patients who benefit from highly qualified and advanced care in the field of vascular surgery.
