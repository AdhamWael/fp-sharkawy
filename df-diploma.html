Message from the Board

This diploma reflects our mission to improve outcomes in diabetic foot management through structured, internationally standardized education.

By integrating theoretical modules, clinical exposure, and hands-on workshops, participants gain the expertise to save limbs and lives. Collaboration between Egyptian and international institutions ensures graduates are prepared to lead multidisciplinary teams worldwide.
